Canapés in Bath and Bristol

Whether you require a light bite to occupy your guests during wedding photographs, or to open a dinner party or corporate event, Taste Buds Catering can deliver a range of canapés that are sure to tantalise your guests. Our handmade canapés are freshly prepared by our highly skilled chefs and delivered to your door.

If required, this can be accompanied by a glass of champagne served by our highly trained event team to provide the perfect finishing touch to any stylish event, ensuring you won’t have to lift a finger. Order canapés in Bath or Bristol by calling Taste Buds today.
