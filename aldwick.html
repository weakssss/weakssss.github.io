Aldwick Court Farm & Vineyard

Aldwick Court Farm & Vineyard is a stunning family run venue hidden away in the Mendip Countryside, surrounded by 300 acres of fields and woodland, with a 17th century farmhouse at it’s centre. Despite the glorious countryside surroundings the venue is only 20 minutes by car from Bristol City Centre.

There are 2 converted barn buildings available for hire on site- both of which are full of character. The ‘Old Barn’ is a 17th century farm building which has been renovated to a very high standard, and has been housing successful functions for over 20 years. The ‘New Barn’ is a relatively new addition to the venue and has been built to complement the existing outbuildings perfectly using reconditioned stone and hand hewn timber beams. The interior design of both barns has been carefully considered to feature clean lines and a welcoming neutral décor.

A unique feature of the farm are the 2 vineyards in the grounds, which were established between 2008 and 2010. The wines produced originate from 6 different grape varieties grown on site, and Aldwick Vineyard currently offer still white, rose and a delicious sparkling blush.

In addition to weddings, Aldwick Court Farm & Vineyard also caters for private events and business functions. Venue hire includes the lawns, gardens and terrace, including terrace tables, chairs and parasols, as well as a fully licensed bar. Our venue manager will be on hand to oversee your event and there is car parking available. The new barn can hold a maximum of 225 guests (with a minimum booking requirement of 60).

Should any of the guests require overnight accommodation, The Forge is a newly refurbished one bedroom apartment available on site for bed and breakfast. There are also numerous other hotels and B&Bs within a 2 mile radius of the venue.
