Business Lunches in Bristol and Bath

Taste Buds Catering in Bristol have the expertise to provide a reliable, cost effective service for all kinds of corporate entertaining, from corporate events to business lunches in Bristol, Bath and the South West.

Whether you require a simple finger buffet or an exceptional three course boardroom meal, we can produce the perfect menu for you.

Choose from canapés, finger buffets, hot or cold knife & fork buffets, formal dinners, a hog roast, barbeque, or anything in between and our professional team will happily spend time working out a catering solution that best suits your needs. To book corporate catering or a business lunch in Bristol call Taste Buds today.

Need a venue for your event?

Taste Buds Catering can also recommend a number of local venues for hire if you so wish, in particular Aldwick Court Farm & Vineyard is a charming venue nestled in the Mendip Hills, a mere 15 minute drive from the centre of Bristol.
