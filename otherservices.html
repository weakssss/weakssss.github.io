Venues

Aldwick Court Farm & Vineyard
A beautiful venue nestling at the foot of the Mendip Hills, between Bristol and Bath. In addition to weddings, Aldwick Court Farm & Vineyard also caters for private events and business functions. A unique feature of the farm are the 2 vineyards in the grounds. The wines produced originate from 6 different grape varieties grown on site, and Aldwick Vineyard currently offer still white, rose and a delicious sparkling blush Find out more
Guildhall, Bath
The Guildhall is situated in the very heart of Bath opposite Bath Abbey. It is a perfect venue for dinners, weddings and conferences. The Banqueting Room with its three large crystal chandeliers is a spectacular and ornate Georgian room. The adjoining Aix-en Provence Room is a versatile antechamber to the main Banqueting Room and can be used for meetings, drinks receptions or used as a bar. In addition smaller rooms are also available to hire for drinks receptions or meetings. www.bathvenues.co.uk
