Caterers In Bristol, Bath & The South West

Taste Buds Catering is a family run company that specialises in providing freshly prepared, quality catering for all occasions in and around Bristol and Bath. At the core of our philosophy is a passion for fresh food, using the best local ingredients and suppliers.

Whether you require a finger buffet, hot or cold knife & fork buffet, canapés, a formal dinner, barbeque or hog roast, Taste Buds can help you. As one of the leading caterers in Bristol, we have the expertise and flexibility to deliver a professional service seven days a week for all private occassions and corporate functions; including anniversary parties,weddings, corporate events, christenings, funerals, dinner parties, and business lunches.

At Taste Buds Catering we understand how crucial it is for your event to run smoothly, so we will work with you to design a personalised menu that suits your taste and budget to ensure that your event is a truly memorable occasion.

If you are looking for professional caterers in Bristol, please explore our website for a taster of our menus. Alternatively call or email and we will be delighted to tailor-make a menu especially for you.
